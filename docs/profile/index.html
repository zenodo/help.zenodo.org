    Profile

    The profile allows you to manage your account and settings.

    • Edit profile — Modify your username, full name, affiliations and/or email address.
    • Change password — Explains how to modify your password.
    • Reset password — Forgot your password?
    • Change profile visibility — Ensure other users can find you on Zenodo.
    • Linking your GitHub/ORCID/OpenAIRE account — Manage which external accounts you can login with on Zenodo.
    • Manage notification preferences — Manage which emails you receive from Zenodo.
    • View logged in devices — Manage all devices that are logged into your account.

    Access your profile

    1

    You access the profile menu from the top-right corner of the header (you must be logged in). Click e.g. the Profile menu item.

    2

    You will be presented with the form to manage your profile.
