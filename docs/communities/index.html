    Communities

    Want to test Zenodo? Use our sandbox environment on https://sandbox.zenodo.org to play.

    Communities are shared areas on Zenodo where projects, institutions, domains, and conferences can curate and manage their research outputs. Communities provides features for managing members, reviewing submissions and curating content in the community.

    Overview

    In this section, learn about the following topics:

      • Add about/curation policy pages — Learn about to provide an about and curation policy text page for your community.
      • Delete community — Learn how to delete a community.
    • Manage members — Learn how to invite members, manage roles and transfer ownership.
      • Invite member — Learn how to invite members to your community.
      • View invitations — Learn how to see the invitations you have sent as community manager.
      • Cancel/edit invitations — Learn how to cancel or change an invitation after it was sent.
      • Remove member — Learn how to remove a member from a community.
      • Leave community — Learn how to leave a community if you no longer wish to be a member.
      • Change member role — Learn how to change the role of an existing member.
      • Set membership visibility — Learn how to set your community membership visibility as a member or community manager.
      • Transfer ownership — Learn transfer ownership of your community.
