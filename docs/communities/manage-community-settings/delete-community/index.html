    Delete community

    TL;DR: Deleting a community CANNOT BE UNDONE and the identifier CANNOT BE REUSED.

    Instead of deleting a community you can transfer ownership of the community to another person. See how you manage members to invite a person and change their role to owner.

    Delete a community

    1

    Go to your community settings. In the danger zone on the bottom of the page, click the Delete community button.

    2

    Confirm that you wish to permanently delete the community by:

    • a) ticking all checkboxes, and
    • b) typing the identifier of the community in the text field and
    • c) clicking the red button Permanently delete.

    The button will be enabled once you have completed (a) and (b).
