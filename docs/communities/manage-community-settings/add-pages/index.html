    Add about/curation policy pages

    A community can provide two text pages which will be linked in the community tab menu:

    • Curation policy
    • About

    In this section you can learn about:

    • Add an about page
    • Add a curation policy page
    • Remove a page

    Add an about page

    You can use the about page to provide more extensive information about your community.

    1

    Go to your community settings and click Pages in the left menu.

    2

    Under About page section fill in the text field with your content. Basic formatting is supported.

    2

    Click Save-button to persist the changes.

    Add a curation policy page

    You can use the curation policy page to announce to other users what are your criteria for accepting submissions to the community.

    1

    Go to your community settings and click Pages in the left menu.

    2

    Under Curation policy section fill in the text field with your content. Basic formatting is supported.

    2

    Click Save-button to persist the changes.

    Remove a page

    1

    Go to your community settings and click Pages in the left menu.

    2

    Clear the text field of the of the page you'd like to remove.

    2

    Click Save-button to persist the changes.
